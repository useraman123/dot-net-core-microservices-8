we also have to register our grpc into docker override



basket.api:
    environment:
      - ASPNETCORE_ENVIRONMENT=Development
      - "CacheSetting__ConnectionString=basketdb:6379"
      - "GrpcSettings__DiscountUrl=http://localhost:8002"--> Grpc resolves from here  (listening to port 8002)
    depends_on: 
     - basketdb
    ports:
      - "8001:8080"
